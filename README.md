# Cafe Sales Data Cleaning and Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on cleaning and analyzing a dirty cafe sales dataset to extract meaningful insights. The goal is to preprocess raw sales data, handle missing values, correct data types, remove inconsistencies, and then perform exploratory data analysis to understand sales trends, popular items, and revenue generation.

## Data Source
The dataset used is `dirty_cafe_sales.csv`, sourced from Kaggle.

## Data Cleaning Steps
The following steps were performed to clean and prepare the data for analysis:
1.  **Initial Inspection**: Loaded the data and inspected its structure and initial missing values.
2.  **Column Removal**: Dropped irrelevant columns such as 'Transaction ID', 'Payment Method', and 'Location' as they were not essential for sales analysis or had a high number of missing values.
3.  **Data Type Conversion**: Convert 'Quantity', 'Price Per Unit', and 'Total Spent' columns to numeric types, coercing errors to `NaN` for further handling. 'Transaction Date' was converted to datetime.
4.  **Missing Value Imputation**:
    *   Missing numeric values in 'Quantity', 'Price Per Unit', and 'Total Spent' were imputed with their respective median values.
    *   Missing values in the 'Item' column were imputed with the mode (most frequent item).
5.  **Handling Inconsistent 'Item' Entries**: Removed rows where 'Item' was classified as 'ERROR' or 'UNKNOWN' to focus on valid product sales.
6.  **Duplicate Removal**: Identified and removed duplicate rows in the DataFrame to ensure data integrity and accurate aggregation.
7.  **Recalculation of Metrics**: Re-calculated all sales metrics (total quantity sold, average spent, monthly sales, top items) after duplicates were removed to ensure accuracy.

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Key Insights
After cleaning, the following analyses were performed:
*   **Total Quantity Sold by Item**: Calculated the total quantity of each item sold to identify best-selling products.
*   **Average Total Spent per Item**: Determined the average revenue generated by individual items.
*   **Monthly Sales Trend**: Visualized the total sales revenue over different months to identify any seasonal patterns or trends.
*   **Top 5 Selling Items by Total Revenue**: Identified the items that contributed the most to the cafe's overall revenue.
